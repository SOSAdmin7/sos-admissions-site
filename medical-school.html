Medical School Application Timeline

Understanding the timeline helps you plan and stay ahead of deadlines.

12-18 Months Before

Planning & Preparation

Identify your target schools, build your activity list, secure letter writers, and develop your personal narrative.

January - April

MCAT Preparation

Take or retake the MCAT. Most students aim to complete testing by April for best school list options.

May - June

Primary Application

AMCAS opens in May. Submit your primary application as early as possible - ideally in the first two weeks.

July - September

Secondary Essays

Schools send secondaries within weeks of verification. Quick turnaround (2 weeks or less) demonstrates interest.

September - March

Interviews

Interview season begins in September. Prepare thoroughly - this is your chance to bring your application to life.

March - April

Decision

Schools release decisions through April 30. You'll need to narrow down to one school by then.
