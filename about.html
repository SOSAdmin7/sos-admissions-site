About Interview SOS

Personalized preparation that helps you interview with confidence. Since 2010.

Meet Vijay, Founder of Interview SOS

Founder & Lead Coach

Vijay founded Interview SOS in 2010 with a simple mission: help nervous, unprepared candidates become confident, polished interviewees. What started as one coach helping friends and family members has grown into a team of experts who have coached thousands of clients.

Vijay has personally coached thousands of clients for medical school interviews, MBA interviews, and job interviews. As a former member of medical school admissions committees, Vijay knows exactly what evaluators are looking for because he's been on the other side of the table.

Today, Interview SOS serves clients worldwide via video conference, phone, and in-person at our Los Angeles office.
