About Interview SOS

Since 2010, we've helped hundreds of job seekers and thousands of students successfully navigate competitive interviews. Some call us "miracle workers."

Our Story

Interview SOS was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: help nervous, unprepared candidates become confident, polished interviewees. What started as one coach helping friends and family members has grown into a team of experts who have coached thousands of clients.

Our coaches have sat on admissions committees at top medical schools, conducted corporate interviews at Fortune 500 companies, and served as alumni interviewers for Ivy League universities. We know exactly what interviewers are looking for because we've been on the other side of the table.

Today, Interview SOS serves clients worldwide via Skype, Zoom, FaceTime, phone, and in-person at our Los Angeles office. Whether you're preparing for a job interview, medical school interview, or college admissions interview, we're here to help you succeed.

Why Choose Us?
