The Multiple Mini Interview (MMI) consists of 6-10 short interview stations (typically 8 minutes each) where you respond to different scenarios, ethical questions, or role-play situations. Many schools now use this format including UCLA, Stanford, and others. If any of your schools use MMI, we strongly recommend our MMI prep package.

We recommend starting 1-2 weeks before your first interview. This gives you time to develop strong answers and practice without the material feeling rehearsed. However, we've successfully prepared clients in as little as 48 hours when needed.
